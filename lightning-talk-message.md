## Body — copy from the rendered preview

---

Hi all,

A few quick things:

**1. Lightning talks.** If there's time during the workshop, would you be interested in giving a 5-minute lightning talk? Pop into your profile on the dashboard ([mira.science/dashboard](https://mira.science/dashboard) → click your name → Edit Profile) and let us know — Yes or No.

**2. Last call for profiles.** While you're in there, this is your last chance to fill out your profile. Share what you're working on, the projects worth checking out, who inspires you, the sidequests you've been on — it's how the rest of the group will get to know your work before we're all in the same room.

**3. Session recordings.** Recordings from all the sessions so far are now on the dashboard under the **"Resources"** tab. Catch up on anything you missed.

Thanks!
— The MIRA Team
